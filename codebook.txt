subject
integer of 30 different test subjects

activity
a factor of six different activities ("WALKING", "WALKING_UPSTAIRS", "WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS", "SITTING", "STANDING", "LAYING")

The means of accelerometer and gyroscope 3-axial raw signals.
